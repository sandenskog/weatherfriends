Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

What data we collect

FriendsCast collects the following data to provide the service:

  • Account information: Your name and email address (via Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, or Facebook Login)
  • Friend list: Names and cities of friends you add
  • Location data: City-level location for weather lookups (we do not track precise GPS coordinates)
  • Chat messages: Messages sent between you and your friends within the app

How we use your data

  • To display weather information for your friends' locations
  • To send weather notifications you've opted into
  • To enable chat between friends

Third-party services

  • Apple WeatherKit: Provides weather data. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and messaging. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Data retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's profile settings.
