FriendsCast lets you see the weather where your friends are — in real time. Add friends, check their local conditions, and never wonder again if they're freezing or sweating.

Features:
• See temperature, wind, humidity and weather conditions for all your friends
• Ranked categories: Hottest, Coldest, Windiest, Wettest
• Mark up to 6 favorites for quick access and home screen widgets
• Map view showing friends around the world
• Chat with friends about the weather
• Daily weather notifications for your favorites
• Weather alerts for extreme conditions

Built with Apple WeatherKit for accurate, up-to-date forecasts worldwide.
